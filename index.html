✨ Our Story
Why TruePrep Exists

Every aspirant deserves a mentor who has actually walked the path. Not algorithms. Not strangers on YouTube. A real person. Their real story.

The Problem We Saw

India has millions of exam aspirants — UPSC, NEET, JEE, SSC, banking — grinding every single day. But the guidance available to most of them? Broken.

🤖
AI gives generic plans
ChatGPT gives you a "study plan" — same for everyone. It doesn't know your weak areas, your city, your background.
📺
YouTube is random
1,000 videos, 500 strategies, zero personalisation. You watch for 3 hours and still don't know what to do tomorrow.
🎭
"Reels gyan" doesn't work
Motivational reels spike your dopamine for 5 minutes. Then you're back to square one. Inspiration ≠ preparation.
👴
Chacha ki advice = outdated
Uncle who cleared SSC in 1998 doesn't know the current syllabus, exam pattern, or what resources actually work today.
💸
Coaching is too expensive
₹1–2 lakhs for coaching where your batch has 200 students. There's no "personal" in that.
🧊
Bhaiya-Didi advice = biased
Well-meaning, but often overfit to their own experience. What worked for them in their city may not work for you.

💡 Our Solution: Human Intelligence, Personally Delivered

We don't automate advice. We connect you with a verified human — someone who has actually cleared your exam — and let them diagnose your specific situation, one-on-one.

The issue is diagnosed personally. The solution is personalised. The accountability is real. That's TruePrep.

Our Vision

We believe India's best exam mentors are not in Delhi coaching centres — they're serving as IAS officers in Jharkhand, practising as doctors in Maharashtra, arguing cases in the Supreme Court. They just never had a platform to reach you.

  • Every aspirant should have access to someone who has been exactly where they are
  • Guidance should be personal, not mass-produced
  • A mentor's real story is more powerful than any lecture
  • Geography should never limit access to quality guidance
  • Affordable should not mean impersonal

What Makes Us Different

We automate the platform — not the mentorship. Our technology makes it easy to find, book, and connect with mentors. But every session is a real human being, giving you their real experience, personalised to your specific situation.

We verify every mentor rigorously. We monitor quality continuously. We keep costs low so this is accessible to every aspirant — not just those who can afford ₹1 lakh coaching.

🔍 Mentor Selection
How We Choose Our Mentors

Not everyone who cleared an exam can be a good mentor. We look for knowledge, communication, and genuine intent to help. Here's exactly how we do it.

01
📄 Document Verification
Every mentor must submit official documentary proof — rank letters, scorecards, degree certificates, government ID. Zero exceptions. Zero self-certification. If the paper doesn't verify, they don't qualify.
02
🎙️ In-Depth Expert Interview
Every candidate is personally interviewed by our team — not an automated test. We assess subject depth, ability to simplify complex concepts, communication quality, and most importantly: their genuine desire to help aspirants.
03
🧪 Trial Session Review
Before any mentor goes live, they conduct a complete mock session that is reviewed by our quality team. We assess structure, depth, empathy, and whether aspirants would genuinely benefit. Only those who pass this are approved.
04
⭐ Continuous Rating Monitoring
Every session is rated by aspirants. Any mentor who falls below 4.0 stars is flagged, reviewed, and if needed, removed from the platform. Quality is not a one-time check — it's ongoing.

Our Promise to Every Aspirant

When you book a session on TruePrep, you are speaking to someone who has actually cleared your exam and has been verified by us at every step. No imposters. No theoretical advisors. Only real experience.
