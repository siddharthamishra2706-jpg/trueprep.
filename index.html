Our Quality Promise
How We Choose Our Mentors — The 4-Fold Method
We don't let just anyone mentor on TruePrep. Every mentor passes our rigorous 4-step verification before they can guide a single aspirant.
01
📄
Document Verification
Every mentor submits official proof — rank letters, scorecards, degree certificates, and government IDs. No self-certification. No exceptions.
02
🎙️
Expert Interview
Our team interviews every mentor personally. We assess their subject knowledge, communication quality, and ability to simplify concepts for aspirants.
03
🧪
Trial Session Review
Mentors conduct a mock session which is reviewed by our quality team. We check practical guidance quality, empathy, and structure before approving them.
04
⭐
Ongoing Rating Monitor
After onboarding, every mentor is continuously rated by aspirants. Mentors falling below 4.0 are reviewed and removed. Quality is not a one-time check.
